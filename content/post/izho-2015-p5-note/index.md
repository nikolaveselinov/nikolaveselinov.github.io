## The problem

{{< problem >}}
Let $\mathcal{F}\subseteq\mathscr{P}(n)$ be a family of sets such that for any $A,B\in\mathcal{F}$ we have $A\cup B\in\mathcal{F}$. Then
$$
\max_{i\in[n]}\tilde d_i\geq\frac{1}{2}\text{.}
$$
